Images of Change - Climate Change: Vital Signs of the Planet

This website from NASA shows aerial images of different areas before and after the effects of climate change in recent areas. The middle slider line reveals a certain amount of each side based on user drag input, and the arrows on the side allow you to switch location. Overall, this site is a very interactive and unique experience that powerfully visualizes of climate change's effects and leaves a lasting impression on the users.
